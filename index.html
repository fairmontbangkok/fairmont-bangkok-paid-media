Why we reduce bids
Plain English: Google Ads works like an auction. Every time someone searches, advertisers bid how much they'll pay per click. By bidding at the minimum recommended rate instead of the average, we pay less per click — so the same ฿100,000 covers more keywords and reaches more people.
Step 01
Google runs an auction
Every search for "luxury hotel Bangkok" triggers a real-time auction. Advertisers bid to appear at the top of results.
Step 02
We bid at the low range
Instead of bidding ฿44/click (avg), we bid ฿13/click (low range). Our ad appears slightly lower on page 1 — but it still shows.
Step 03
Same budget, more reach
Each click costs less, so ฿100K covers 3 keywords across all 4 markets instead of 2. Result: +444% more clicks.
