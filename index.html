Priority recommendations
1
Skew budget toward HK & MY. Lowest CPCs (฿27–฿44). Allocating ฿35K each to HK and MY, with ฿15K to SG and ฿5K to AU gives better coverage and more clicks per baht.
2
Run niche long-tail keywords only. "Sukhumvit 20 hotel" and "hotel near asok station" are the only keywords that approach affordability. Head terms are completely out of range at this budget.
3
Set strict daily caps at ฿833/market. Without a hard daily cap, SG/AU budgets exhaust in the first 2 weeks. Ads go dark for the rest of the month — cap prevents this.
4
Consider concentrating on HK & MY only. At ฿25K each, SG and AU buy only 5–6% of a keyword's full month. Putting ฿50K each into HK and MY gives meaningful niche coverage.
5
Increase budget to ฿500K+ for real impact. To run even one mid-tier keyword (328 daily searches) across all 4 markets, you need ~฿1.9M/month. ฿500K unlocks HK/MY head terms meaningfully.
